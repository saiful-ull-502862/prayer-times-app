About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Systems Engineering (Mechanical Engineering) at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, with over 4 years of hands-on experience in Finite Element Analysis using ABAQUS and Python scripting.

My research focuses on advancing orthopedic biomechanics through the development of multi-scale finite element models of articular cartilage and markerless motion capture frameworks. This work aims to improve osteoarthritis risk prediction and support real-world clinical and military health applications.
